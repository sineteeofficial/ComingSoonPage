About Us

Sinetee is an eLearning platform where we challenge the outdated education system.We plan to help students to invest their time in their hobbies and passion. We want our students to realize the real world out there at their early ages. This platform is to encourage students to develop required skill sets and make them industry ready by the time they start their career in their respective fields. Not just that, we also provide value added training to help them build a responsible person out of them. In future, we are planning to include industrial tour and provide small to full-time internships throughout their learning as we focus more on practical knowledge without compromising with required theoretical knowledge.
